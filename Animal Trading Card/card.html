Wolf

Wolves have over 200 million scent cells. They can hear up to six miles away in the forest. A wolf's jaw has a crushing power of nearly 1500 pounds per square inch! A wolf can eat 20 pounds of meat in one meal.

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus
  • Average Length: 3.4 - 5.2 ft
  • Average Lifespan: 16 years
  • Habitat: Tundra to woodlands, forests, grasslands and deserts.

Gray wolves are canines with bushy tails and gray and brown facial markings and undersides. They look very similar to a German Shepard.
